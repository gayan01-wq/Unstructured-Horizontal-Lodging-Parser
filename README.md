# Unstructured-Horizontal-Lodging-Parser

An advanced hospitality quantitative analytics engine designed to process destination capacity metrics, benchmark competitor room inventories, and track pacing velocity.

## Methodology & Architectural Alignment
This framework models time-series data clusters to diagnose demand shifts. The algorithmic architecture is designed to complement predictive time-series foundation methodologies, specifically aligning with deep-learning forecasting horizons established by frameworks such as Google Research's **TimesFM (Time Series Foundation Model)**.

## Core Metrics Computed
* **Market Share Variance:** Measures actual volume capture against mathematical destination fair share targets.
* **Yield Friction Index:** Continuously evaluates the mathematical elasticity between dynamic rate positioning (ADR) and booking acceleration boundaries.
